Ozy Brennan (né Frantz) is . 

I don't know if you remember how desperate internet gender discourse was around 2015. Cruelty was normal and encouraged. It was impossible to talk. Ozy were notable as being one of the only feminist bloggers to engage with rationalism / one of the only rationalist bloggers to engage with feminism. If you're reading this, chances are you haven't engaged with either set of ideas enough. (It is a cliche in feminist writing that men also suffer under patriarchal norms and would thus benefit from feminism. But it's rare for there to be any real analysis of this. Ozy did it.)
